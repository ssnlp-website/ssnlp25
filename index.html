SSNLP 2025

The 2025 Singapore Symposium on Natural Language Processing

Welcome!

We are excited to announce the Singapore Symposium on Natural Language Processing 2025 (SSNLP'25) will take place on Wednesday, April 23, 2025, as a full-day event. SSNLP is a premier platform for Principal Investigators (PIs) in Singapore to present ongoing and upcoming work, fostering collaboration and receiving valuable international feedback. We welcome poster submissions on all aspects of Natural Language Processing (NLP). This is an excellent opportunity for both faculties and students to gain international exposure and engage with leading experts in the field.

We welcome submissions on all aspects of Natural Language Processing (NLP). This is an excellent opportunity for both faculties and students to gain international exposure and engage with leading experts in the field. The submission deadline is February 28, 23:59, 2025!
